Terms of Use & Privacy

Pilot Version — By Invitation Only

What GroundLog is

GroundLog is a field compliance logging tool for environmental inspection work. It is currently in beta and available by invitation only. It is not a commercial product and is provided as-is for pilot testing.

What data we collect

When you use GroundLog, we collect and store:

  • Your email address and account credentials (via Google Firebase Authentication)
  • Field log entries you create (daily logs, compliance notes, timesheet records)
  • Photos you upload, including GPS coordinates embedded in photo metadata
  • Field map markers and KML layers you import
  • Project configuration you enter (project name, organization, contractor)

All data is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure under your individual account. No data is shared between users.

How we use your data

Your data is used only to operate the app for you. We do not share your data with third parties, use it for advertising, sell or license it in any form, or train AI models on it.

AI report generation uses the Anthropic Claude API. Field log content is sent to the API to generate your report and is not retained by Anthropic after the response is returned, per Anthropic's API data policy.

Your data, your responsibility

GroundLog is a logging and documentation tool. You are responsible for the accuracy and completeness of what you enter. GroundLog-generated reports are drafts — always review before submitting to any agency or client.

No warranty

GroundLog is provided as-is during this pilot period. There are no guarantees of uptime, data retention, or fitness for any particular purpose. Do not use GroundLog as your only record of compliance-critical information.

Changes and termination

This is a pilot. Features will change. Access may be revoked at any time. You will be notified of any significant changes to these terms.

Contact

Questions or concerns: support@groundlog.io

Governing law

These terms are governed by the laws of the State of Pennsylvania.
